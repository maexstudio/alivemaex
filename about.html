About

the world, the sound, and everything press & bookers need.

the world

An old soul speaking from inside a modern body.

ALIVEMAEX lives somewhere between Berlin nights, old records and a future that arrived too quickly. Close vocals, warm machines, heavy drums and broken echoes.

Music about distance, desire and the moments in which people almost let each other in.

Liebe über Angst.

made by hand

The current world of ALIVEMAEX is written, recorded, produced, mixed and mastered by me. In earlier years, some records were finished with a mastering engineer — today every step happens in my own space. I care deeply about the craft — the weight of a drum, the distance of a voice, the moment a sound finally feels alive.

The music is a way of giving shape to things I cannot explain in words. This world is open. Come in. Stay a while.
